Accelerate Your Growth with Ultrahack

Ultrahack believes in creating your own future! We connect talents, startups, corporates and public organizations to collaborate and create future innovations. We drive open innovation through our end-to-end model that maximize the impact of your innnovation!

Pre-event prep

Familiarise yourself with the challenges, and come up with an innovative solution or apply with your existing project. Register yourself, submit your project, add your team and continue developing your project prior to the event. Keep updating your submission content and team. Finalists are selected from the moment the challenge opens... so act fast!

The Hack

An Onsite or Online intensive project development period supported by knowledgeable and skilled mentors to finalise the demo of your solution. Its also a great opportunity to network and learn new skills. Pitch to the jury to win!!!

Innovate, Incubate, Accelerate

Even if you arent chosen as a winner, we encourage you to continue with your project. Many partners or other entities may be interested in future possibilities with teams so be active, keep the communication active with challenge partners, community partners and hack your future.

Hackathons

02

HACK CHALLENGES FROM LEADING INDUSTRIES

Leading industries, public sector organizations and NGOs present their challenges for you to hack. Kick off your project on our platform during the pre-game period and continue to the 48-hour hackathon event.

The pre-game period gives you more time to develop your solution, get feedback, recruit new team members and get expert mentoring. At the hackathon event the pre-selected teams have 48 hours to develop their solutions round-by-round and pitch them to partner judges and investors.

Check out challenges and prizes, register to the platform and apply to the hackathon of your choice!

WHAT IS YOUR STRENGTH?

Whether you are an individual developer, an aspiring startup entrepreneur or a corporation looking for the insight of the hacker community you are welcome to join!

Ultrahack is open to all skillsets and ideas: senior and junior developers, teams and individuals, corporate teams and students, ideas and projects.

We believe that talent from all walks of life makes our community unique and helps to develop more comprehensive solutions. We also believe hacking means continuous learning, which is why we encourage students and hackers-to-be apply.

Immerse yourself in networking, make your skills useful to others and accept the challenge try out something you didnt dare to do before.

JOIN ULTRAHACK AS...

HACKER

At Ultrahack we invite you to test your limits - be it an intensive coding marathon or a jump into unknown as you take a chance at developing your dream project.

VOLUNTEER

At Ultrahack we appreciate the spirit of helping each other in any way we can. We are Yes, I can do that!-type of people. Apply for volunteering at Ultrahack!

PARTNER

Whether your organization is looking for solutions to your business challenges, promoting your brand to thousands of innovators or learning to leverage open innovation, we have a service for you. Ultrahack is your long-term partner in open innovation.

STARTUP

Are you startup looking for a market access, pilot partner or seed funding? Ultrahack will be the most productive weekend of the year. Pitch your solution, get feedback and benefit from our mentoring. Meet potential customers, partners or co-founders.

Reasons to become an Ultrahacker

04

Reasons to become an Ultrahacker

  • Fast track to success
    If you want to make it big, our startup coaches, world-class mentors combined with funding opportunities help you to get there. Our accelerator partners offer investments to promising Ultrahack teams. Are you one of them?
  • Win valuable prizes
    Its not all about money, but it doesnt hurt to have some. So far, Ultrahack teams have earned + 200,000 euros as prize money. And we aim to keep up the good work!
  • Get found
    Ultrahack is buzzing with action. You never know wholl you meet. Your potential customer, employee or co-founder might be sitting just behind the corner!
  • Step into limelight
    Pitch your project to investors and industry partners and receive feedback from experienced mentors and fellow hackers. And the Media will write about you!
  • Perfect your skills
    Whether its your coding skills or you strive to sharpen your business instinct Ultrahack helps to take your skills to a new level. Join our pre-events and workshops to get ahead in the competition.
  • Take up challenges that really matter
    We create challenges in collaboration with leading industries and organizations. Your solution could help to pave way for better future.
  • Get your hands on the latest tech and APIs
    Ultrahack partners open up their technology reserves and APIs for your use. As a tech savvy developer, this is something you really can not miss!
  • Go global
    Ultrahack community stretches beyond one region and location. Consider online participation or applying for our travel grants.
  • Retain your IPR rights
    Your IPR rights will always belong to you and you are free to discuss about commercializing those to our challenges partners if they are worth it!
  • Earn study credits
    Several universities offer ECTS credits for your participation in Ultrahack! Progress in your studies and earn credits by following the instructions given by your university or contact your teacher for additional info.\
